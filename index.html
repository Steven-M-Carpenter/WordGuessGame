The Gallows - A Word Guess Game

Instructions

Select a letter to see if it appears in the random word chosen from a 150 item word list. If you are correct, the letter appears in the proper sequence. If you are wrong, the chose a new letter until you are out of turns. Your turns start 5 higher than the lenght of the word.

Good Luck!
